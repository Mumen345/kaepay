Kae Pay’s mission is to empower individuals through a customized, cost-effective, and efficient FinTech platform. We focus on boosting financial literacy, streamlining transactions, and supporting entrepreneurial efforts, all while promoting financial independence and sustainable growth. With Kae Pay, you can seamlessly manage your finances, payments, and transactions from one integrated platform. Kae Pay is your comprehensive solution for all payment needs and more.


Founded in 2024 and backed by Nedbank and Ukeshe, we are dedicated to promoting financial independence and sustainable growth through innovative technology and fostering shared prosperity. Kae Pay is a comprehensive financial service platform that offers payments, transfers, and essential services. Its integrated marketplace creates a financially inclusive environment, equipping every student with the tools and opportunities needed to excel both academically and economically.
